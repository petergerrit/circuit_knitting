CIRCUIT_UTILS DIRECTORY
========================

This directory contains Python modules for quantum circuit analysis and visualization.

FILES:

statevector_analysis.py
- Performs ideal statevector simulations
- Computes theoretical probabilities of circuit outcomes
- Provides reference data for circuit knitting validation

show_circuits.py
- Displays quantum circuit diagrams
- Shows circuit structure and measurement outcomes
- Saves text representations of circuits

show_circuit_ratios.py
- Analyzes measurement outcomes as ratios
- Compares theoretical vs actual circuit behavior
- Uses statevector analysis for ideal probabilities

PURPOSE:
These utilities provide visualization and theoretical analysis to complement
the core circuit knitting functionality. They help validate, debug, and
understand quantum circuit behavior through ideal simulations.
